Thrissur is called the Cultural Capital of Kerala because it is famous for its festivals, art, music, and traditions. The most popular festival here is Thrissur Pooram. It is a big temple festival with decorated elephants, drums, and fireworks. Many people from all over the world come to see it. Thrissur also has many important places to visit like Vadakkumnathan Temple, churches, museums, and palaces. It is also known for its gold jewellery, Ayurveda medicine, and classical dance schools. Thrissur is also home to Kerala Kalamandalam, a famous school where people learn classical dance and music like Kathakali and Mohiniyattam. These are traditional art forms that are very special to Kerala.
